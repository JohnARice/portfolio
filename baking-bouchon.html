Baking Bouchon WordPress Blog

The Bouchon Bakery is chef Thomas Keller’s line of bakeries in the United States. Thomas Keller has released a cookbook also named Bouchon Bakery with some of the bakery’s recipes. A friend of mine is going to bake his way through the book and post each recipe to a blog as he completes them. This is the blog I designed for him.
