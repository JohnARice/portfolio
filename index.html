I'm a data-driven digital marketer with over 10 years experience in creating marketing campaigns that deliver leads and significantly increase a company’s bottom line. With a proven track record in both B2B and B2C digital marketing, I research, align and optimize your company’s digital marketing strategy and tactics to your company’s business and marketing goals. I am a graduate of Sheridan College’s Marketing Management program and, in 2017, I will graduate from the University of Toronto’s Digital Marketing Management program.

I translate your company's business requirements and user needs into websites and landing pages that convert and drive business. Focused on front-end development, I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, jQuery, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Foundation. The content management systems I’ve used include both SharePoint and WordPress. I am also graduate of Humber College’s Web Design, Development and Maintenance certificate.

Using research and analytics, I deliver user experiences tailored to your company’s end user needs and business requirements. Some the UX techniques I use include personas, card sorting, information architecture, site maps, wireframes, customer journey maps, prototyping and usability testing. My UX tools include Omnigraffle, Balsamiq, Axure, Sketch, Invision and Optimizely. In 2016, I earned the User Experience Design Certificate from George Brown College.

Using project management techniques and methodologies, I deliver marketing campaigns and websites that are on time, on budget and within scope. I’m experienced in both the waterfall and agile project management methodologies. In 2012, I earned a Certificate in Project Management from Humber College.
